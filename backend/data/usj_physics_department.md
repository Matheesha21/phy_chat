# USJ Physics Department

## About

The Department of Physics of the University of Sri Jayewardenepura has a long history that runs back to more than five decades. The Department is among the largest, in view of the number of students within the Faculty of Applied Sciences. It occupies two spacious two-story buildings that house lecturing theatres, halls and research laboratories. Falling in line with the mission of the University and the Faculty, the Department of Physics strives to provide a high quality learning experience to the students through a series of course units which cover both traditional fundamental aspects of Physics as well as more exciting recent developments.

The Department of Physics is proud of its student achievements. Our graduates have secured high profile careers as University academics, Scientists, Administrative officers, Managers, Teachers etc. and are contributing to uplift the productivity and economic growth of the nation.

## ELETTRA User Institute

University of Sri Jayewardenepura has become a User Institute at a Central Research Facility in Europe.

ELETTRA is an international multidisciplinary research center of excellence, specialized in synchrotron (SR) and free electron laser (FEL) radiation and their applications in materials and life sciences, situated in Trieste, Italy. Synchrotron is a huge scientific machine designed to produce high brilliance high flux tunable X-ray and ultra violet (UV) radiation in a wide range of frequency and photon energy using relativistic electrons in circular motion. This "Synchrotron Light" can penetrate deep inside matter and allows scientists to investigate the world around us at the scale of atoms and molecules.

SR beamtime for experiments in fundamental as well as applied research can be accessed by scientists from all over the world through their respective institutes with funding secured from various national/international agencies in Europe. In order to apply for funding, the respective institute must become registered as a User Institute at ELETTRA. All leading universities and research institutes in the United States (US), United Kingdom (UK), Europe and Asia-Australasia, with researchers working in cutting-edge thematic areas of research, have become registered User Institutes at ELETTRA, which earns them a world reputation and a parity status as institutes steering research activities on topics with global interest.

The Department of Physics, Faculty of Applied Sciences, University of Sri Jayewardenepura, is glad to announce that the University has become a registered User Institute at ELETTRA as a result of the liaison of Dr Kumarasinghe with ELETTRA. Dr. Kumarasinghe is currently working at the Department of Physics and has extensive experience in the application of synchrotron radiation in Experimental Condensed Matter Physics related fields, and has been able to secure funding for SR beam times since 2009. Synchrotron beamtime at various beamlines can now be accessed by any researcher working at the University of Sri Jayewardenepura by submitting high quality research proposals to the panel of experts at ELETTRA. Proposals submitted under the general user category undergo a stringent peer review process currently existing in the European Union (EU), and beamtime for experiments will be granted only for successful applicants. Once beamtime is secured, funding for experiments can be requested and secured through the Abdus Salam International Centre for Theoretical Physics (ICTP), Trieste, Italy, under the ICTP-ELETTRA User Program, which is dedicated to scientists from the South.
